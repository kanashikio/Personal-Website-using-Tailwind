Destination

Batanes is an archipelagic province comprising ten islands, with three of them being the main inhabited ones: Batan, Sabtang, and Itbayat. It is located at the northernmost tip of Philippine territory, facing the Pacific Ocean to the east and the South China Sea to the west. Batanes is known for its scenic waters and terrain and clean environment.

Food

Sisig is a popular Filipino dish made by boiling, chopping, and grilling parts of pig's head such as ears, cheeks, and jowls, which are then seasoned with salt, pepper, and vinegar. The meat is combined with fried onions, sili, and chicken livers, and the whole concoction is traditionally topped with a raw egg. The name of the dish is believed to derive from the word sisigan, meaning to make sour.
